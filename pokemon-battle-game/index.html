HOW TO PLAY
📍 Enter your name to start the game and play against the computer.
📍 You and the computer will each get assigned three Pokemons (1st generation only). Each Pokemon comes with one attack move to use on the enemy.
📍 Your Pokemon and the computer's Pokemon will take turns attacking each other to deal damage.
📍 At any time during your turn, you can swap in a different Pokemon by clicking on the small card. Choose those that are strong against the enemy Pokemon types!
📍 The Pokemon will faint after its HP is exhausted.
📍 The player that causes the enemy's all three Pokemons to faint wins!
